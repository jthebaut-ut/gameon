OVERVIEW

This Privacy Policy describes how FanGeo Sports ("FanGeo," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and retains information when you use the FanGeo mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. WHO WE ARE

FanGeo Sports operates FanGeo, a sports discovery and fan community app that helps users find venues, games, pickup activity, live match information, and social features such as Fan Chats and direct messages. Business accounts may manage venue listings and hosted games.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Account and profile information

When you create or use a Fan account or business account, we may collect:

- Email address and authentication identifiers (including Sign in with Apple credentials and, where applicable, Apple private relay email addresses)
- Display name, username (@handle), bio, and profile avatar
- Account type (fan or business), account status, and membership or entitlement status
- Favorite teams, national team identity preferences, home crowd venue selection, and related fan identity preferences
- Live visibility settings, discoverability preferences, and notification settings
- Friend connections, blocks, and social interaction metadata
- Last activity timestamps used for presence and service operation

2.2 User-generated content

We process content you create or submit, including:

- Direct messages and private conversation metadata
- Fan Chats and venue event comments
- Pickup game listings, join requests, invites, and related messages
- Game attendance signals (for example, Going or Interested), saved games, predictions, and ratings where available
- Reports you submit and optional report details
- Support requests submitted through the app
- Profile photos and venue or business photos you upload
- Venue and business listing information, game schedules, and claim submissions

2.3 Location and discovery information

With your permission, FanGeo may access your device location while you use the app to center the map on your current location and show nearby sports venues and activity. If you do not grant location permission, you may still browse using map regions, searched cities, venue addresses, and coordinates associated with listings or events you view.

Pickup game locations may be set using a map pin and address fields. Venue and business listings include addresses and coordinates used for discovery and distance calculations.

2.4 Device, app, and technical information

We may collect information needed to operate and secure the Service, including:

- Device type, operating system version, app version, and language settings
- Push notification device tokens when you enable notifications
- Calendar event identifiers stored locally when you enable Apple Calendar sync
- Diagnostic, performance, and error information used to maintain reliability

2.5 Advertising and consent-related information

FanGeo uses Google AdMob to display banner and native advertisements in certain areas of the app. Depending on your consent choices, device settings, and applicable law, AdMob and its partners may process advertising identifiers (such as IDFA where permitted), device information, ad interaction data, approximate location derived from IP address, and consent signals from Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP).

FanGeo may show a pre-consent notice, UMP consent forms, and Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt before personalized advertising is requested where required.

2.6 First-party analytics

FanGeo maintains first-party analytics stored in our backend. When you are signed in, we may record events such as app opens, activity touches, game creation, game joins, comment posts, and direct messages sent. These events may include user identifiers, entity identifiers, coarse location fields (city, region, country where available), sport context, and limited metadata. We do not use third-party analytics SDKs such as Firebase Analytics in the current app.

2.7 Safety and moderation information

When you report content or users, block another user, or when content is automatically hidden based on report thresholds, we process report records, selected categories or reasons, optional details, reported content snapshots or identifiers, timestamps, account identifiers, moderation status, and related safety records.

3. HOW WE USE INFORMATION

We use information to:

- Create and authenticate accounts and provide fan and business features
- Display profiles, venues, games, pickup activity, live match information, and social content
- Deliver direct messages, Fan Chats, notifications, and calendar sync where enabled
- Operate friend connections, blocking, reporting, moderation, and account enforcement
- Review venue and business claims and manage business listings
- Process Business Pro subscriptions and business entitlements where applicable
- Show advertisements and honor your ad consent choices
- Prevent abuse, enforce our policies, investigate safety issues, and comply with legal obligations
- Improve, maintain, debug, and secure the Service
- Respond to support requests and communicate with you about the Service

4. HOW WE SHARE INFORMATION

4.1 Other users

Information you place in your profile or post publicly in the Service (such as display name, username, bio, avatar, comments, predictions, attendance signals, and pickup listings) may be visible to other users according to your settings and the feature you use.

4.2 Service providers

We use trusted providers to operate the Service, including:

- Supabase for authentication, database, storage, realtime features, edge functions, and push token storage
- Google AdMob for advertising and consent management
- Apple for Sign in with Apple, push notification delivery, in-app purchases, calendar access, and platform services
- Sports data providers such as TheSportsDB for schedules and live match data

These providers process information on our behalf subject to their terms and our instructions where applicable.

4.3 Legal and safety reasons

We may disclose information if we believe it is reasonably necessary to comply with law, respond to lawful requests, protect users or the public, investigate abuse, enforce our Terms of Service or Community Guidelines, or protect the rights and safety of FanGeo Sports.

4.4 Business transfers

If FanGeo Sports is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, or sale of assets, information may be transferred as part of that transaction subject to appropriate protections.

5. YOUR CHOICES AND CONTROLS

- Account settings: You may update profile information, notification preferences, live visibility, discoverability, and related settings in the app.
- Location: You may disable location access for FanGeo in iOS Settings.
- Notifications: You may control notification categories in app settings and iOS notification settings.
- Calendar sync: Apple Calendar integration is off by default and can be enabled or disabled in settings.
- Blocking: You may block other users where blocking is supported in the app.
- Private chat lock: You may optionally require Face ID or device passcode before opening direct messages on your device. This control is stored locally on your device.
- Advertising choices: Where required, you may manage ad consent through UMP forms and, when available, the in-app "Privacy & Ad Choices" setting. You may also limit ad tracking in iOS Settings.
- Account deletion: Fan accounts and business accounts may request deletion in Settings, subject to the disclosures in Section 8.

6. DATA RETENTION

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the Service, maintain safety, resolve disputes, enforce our policies, comply with law, and meet App Store obligations. Retention periods vary by data type.

Some information may remain after account deletion when necessary to preserve conversation integrity, safety records, moderation history, venue or business records, fraud prevention, or legal compliance. Deleted fan accounts may continue to appear as "Deleted User" in certain messages or comments.

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

FanGeo is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information to FanGeo, contact us at support@fangeosports.com and we will take appropriate steps.

8. ACCOUNT DELETION

Fan accounts: When you delete your fan account, we remove or anonymize your public profile and personal preferences, including favorites, saved venues, attendance signals, and avatar images stored for your account. Some direct messages, Fan Chat comments, reports, and moderation records may be retained and displayed as "Deleted User." Deleted accounts cannot sign in again unless FanGeo support restores the account in limited circumstances.

Business accounts: When you delete a business account, business-created venues may be permanently deleted and community venues you claimed may be released according to the in-app deletion flow. Related photos and business records may be removed as described at deletion time.

9. INTERNATIONAL USERS

FanGeo Sports is based in the United States. If you use the Service from outside the United States, your information may be processed in the United States and other countries where our service providers operate. Those countries may have different data protection laws than your country.

10. YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or obtain a copy of certain personal information, object to or restrict certain processing, withdraw consent where processing is based on consent, and lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise privacy rights, contact support@fangeosports.com. We may need to verify your request. Some rights may be limited by law or by information we must retain for safety, legal, or operational reasons.

11. CALIFORNIA PRIVACY NOTICE

California residents may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), as amended by the CPRA, including rights to know, delete, correct, and opt out of certain sharing for cross-context behavioral advertising.

FanGeo uses advertising partners that may process personal information for advertising purposes subject to your consent and device settings. You can limit ad tracking in iOS Settings and manage ad consent through UMP where available. FanGeo does not sell personal information for money. We may share certain identifiers and device information with advertising partners as described in this policy.

California residents may submit requests to support@fangeosports.com.

12. EUROPEAN ECONOMIC AREA, UNITED KINGDOM, AND SWITZERLAND

Where GDPR or UK GDPR applies, our legal bases for processing may include performance of a contract, legitimate interests (such as safety, fraud prevention, and service improvement), consent (for example, location, notifications, advertising, or calendar access where required), and legal obligations.

You may withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent, without affecting the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

13. SECURITY

We use administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

14. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy at https://fangeosports.com/privacy and update the effective date. Material changes may also be communicated in the app where appropriate.
