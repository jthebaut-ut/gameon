COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

FanGeo Sports
Effective date: June 16, 2026
Website: https://fangeosports.com/community-guidelines

---

OVERVIEW

FanGeo is built for sports fans who want to discover games, venues, and local community activity. These Community Guidelines explain the behavior we expect in the app. They apply to all users, including fan accounts, business accounts, and venue representatives. By using FanGeo, you agree to follow these guidelines in addition to our Terms of Service.

1. SPORTS RIVALRY IS WELCOME; ABUSE IS NOT

Debate teams, celebrate wins, talk about the scoreboard, and enjoy friendly rivalry. Do not use rivalry as an excuse to harass, threaten, demean, or target individuals.

2. BE RESPECTFUL

Treat other fans, venue staff, business account holders, and FanGeo support with respect. Disagreement is fine. Personal attacks, bullying, and repeated unwanted contact are not.

3. PROHIBITED CONDUCT AND CONTENT

The following are not allowed on FanGeo:

- Harassment, bullying, threats, intimidation, or targeted abuse
- Hate speech, slurs, racism, discrimination, or dehumanizing language
- Doxxing, sharing private information, or pressuring others to reveal personal details
- Impersonation of users, venues, teams, leagues, public figures, or FanGeo staff
- Spam, scams, phishing, fake promotions, or deceptive listings
- Sexual exploitation, unwanted sexual content, or content involving minors
- Encouraging violence, self-harm, illegal activity, or unsafe meetups
- False or misleading venue, business, game, or attendance information
- Attempts to evade moderation, bans, blocks, or reporting systems

4. FAN CHATS AND COMMENTS

Fan Chats and venue event comments should stay relevant to the game, venue, or local sports conversation.

- Keep comments concise and on topic
- Do not flood threads with spam or repeated disruptive posts
- Do not post profanity, slurs, or sexually explicit content
- Report comments that violate these guidelines using the in-app flag where available

Comments may be hidden, removed, or reviewed when reported or when they appear to violate these guidelines.

5. DIRECT MESSAGES AND FRIENDS

Direct messages are for respectful conversation between users who have accepted a friend connection.

- Do not send abusive, threatening, sexual, scam, or spam messages
- Stop messaging someone who asks you to stop
- Use block and report tools if you feel unsafe or harassed
- Do not use direct messages to solicit unrelated commercial activity or mass outreach

6. PROFILES AND IDENTITY

Your profile should represent you honestly.

- Use a display name, username, bio, and avatar that are not misleading or impersonating others
- Do not use profile fields to attack, harass, or promote scams
- Respect discoverability and live visibility settings of other users

7. PICKUP GAMES AND REAL-WORLD MEETUPS

Pickup games are organized by fans, not supervised by FanGeo.

- Provide accurate game details, location, timing, and expectations
- Respect organizer decisions on join requests and invites
- Do not use pickup games to harass, discriminate, or endanger participants
- Meet in public places, communicate clearly, and prioritize personal safety

FanGeo is not responsible for offline conduct, injuries, disputes, or fees at pickup games.

8. BUSINESS ACCOUNTS AND VENUE LISTINGS

Business accounts must represent authorized venues or businesses.

- Submit accurate addresses, photos, descriptions, amenities, and game schedules
- Do not claim venues you do not represent
- Do not use venue listings for unrelated advertising, scams, or misleading promotions
- Respond professionally in venue-related Fan Chats when engaging as a business representative

FanGeo may review, reject, limit, or remove listings or claims that violate these guidelines or our Terms of Service.

9. PREDICTIONS, ATTENDANCE, AND COMMUNITY SIGNALS

Do not manipulate attendance signals, predictions, ratings, or community features to mislead others, harass venues, or disrupt the Service.

10. REPORTING AND BLOCKING

Use in-app reporting and blocking tools in good faith. False or abusive reporting may itself result in enforcement action.

For issues not covered by in-app tools, contact support@fangeosports.com.

11. ENFORCEMENT

Violations may result in:

- Content removal or hiding
- Feature restrictions
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent account or business account ban
- Preservation of reports and safety records

Enforcement depends on severity, context, and repeat behavior. See our Trust & Safety Policy for more detail.
