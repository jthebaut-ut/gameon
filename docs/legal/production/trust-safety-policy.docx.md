OVERVIEW

FanGeo Sports ("FanGeo," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to helping sports fans connect safely. This Trust & Safety Policy explains how reporting, blocking, moderation, account enforcement, and support work in the FanGeo app. It supplements our Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.

1. OUR SAFETY APPROACH

FanGeo combines community reporting, automated safeguards, human review, and account enforcement to address abusive behavior and harmful content. No system is perfect. Users should use good judgment, especially when interacting offline.

Submitting a report does not automatically ban a user, delete an entire conversation, hide a thread from the reporter, or notify the person reported. Reports are reviewed according to severity, context, repeat behavior, and applicable policy.

2. WHAT YOU CAN REPORT IN THE APP

2.1 Fan Chats and venue event comments

You may flag another user's Fan Chat comment or venue event comment using the in-app flag control. Each account may have one active report per comment. You may remove an accidental report before moderation thresholds apply by tapping the flag again.

When three unique active reports are received on the same comment, the comment may be automatically hidden from public view while it is reviewed. Removing a report after a comment has already been hidden does not automatically restore it.

2.2 Direct messages

From a direct message thread, you may report:

- A user for categories such as spam, harassment, fake account, inappropriate content, or other
- A conversation with category, optional details, consent confirmation, and a selectable review window of up to seven days
- An individual incoming message using the message context menu

Conversation reports are limited to one open report per reporter per conversation. FanGeo may apply cooldowns and duplicate-report limits to prevent abuse of the reporting system.

When three unique reports are received on the same direct message, that message may be automatically removed from view while it is reviewed.

2.3 Venue and business issues

Venue owners may review flagged Fan Chat comments related to their venues through business settings where available. For broader venue listing concerns, unauthorized claims, or business account issues, contact support@fangeosports.com or use the in-app support request form.

2.4 Support requests

Signed-in users may contact FanGeo support through the in-app support form for technical issues, account help, safety concerns, venue support, and billing or other questions. Support requests are rate-limited to prevent abuse.

3. BLOCKING

You may block another user from a direct message thread or manage blocked users from chat settings. Blocking is enforced across supported social surfaces, including direct messaging, friend requests, and other interactions where blocking checks are implemented.

Blocking limits unwanted contact inside FanGeo. It does not prevent someone from contacting you outside the app, and it does not automatically remove an existing friend connection in every case. If you no longer want contact, block the user and stop responding.

4. AUTOMATED SAFEGUARDS

FanGeo uses technical safeguards to reduce abuse, including:

- Client-side profanity filtering on certain submissions
- Rate limits on comments, direct messages, reports, and support requests
- Automatic hiding or removal thresholds for heavily reported comments and messages
- Server-side access controls and row-level security for private messaging and social actions
- Blocking checks on friend requests, messaging, discovery, and related interactions

Automated systems may make mistakes. Users may contact support if they believe action was taken in error.

5. MODERATION REVIEW

When content is reported or automatically flagged, FanGeo or authorized reviewers may review:

- Report category or reason and optional details
- Reported comment, message snapshot, or limited conversation window
- Reporter and reported account identifiers and timestamps
- Moderation history and related safety records

Depending on severity and context, FanGeo may:

- Take no action or dismiss a report
- Hide or delete content
- Warn a user or restrict features
- Suspend or permanently ban a fan account or business account
- Disable an account through administrative status controls
- Preserve records needed for safety, compliance, or dispute resolution

Moderation records may be retained after account deletion where necessary.

6. VENUE OWNER REVIEW TOOLS

Authenticated venue owners may access a flagged comments review area in business settings. Venue owners may delete a reported comment or dismiss individual reports on comments they are authorized to review. Venue owner tools are intended for venue-specific comment moderation and do not replace FanGeo's broader safety review for direct messages and account-level enforcement.

7. ACCOUNT SUSPENSIONS AND BANS

FanGeo may suspend or ban accounts for serious or repeated violations of our Terms of Service or Community Guidelines, for legal compliance, or to protect users and the Service.

When an account is suspended or banned, the user may see an in-app account suspension screen and may be prevented from using core features until the suspension ends or the ban is lifted. Business accounts may be subject to separate business enforcement controls.

Users who believe an enforcement action was a mistake may contact support@fangeosports.com.

8. ACCOUNT DELETION AND SAFETY RECORDS

Users may delete fan accounts or business accounts in Settings. Fan account deletion anonymizes the public profile and removes personal preferences, but some messages, Fan Chat comments, reports, and moderation records may remain and appear as "Deleted User" where needed to preserve conversation integrity and safety records.

Deleted accounts are blocked from signing in again unless FanGeo support restores the account in limited circumstances.

9. PRIVACY, ADS, AND SENSITIVE CONTROLS

FanGeo offers optional device-level protection for direct messages using Face ID or device passcode before opening chats. This control is stored on your device and is separate from server moderation.

Advertising is provided through Google AdMob. Where required, FanGeo presents consent and tracking choices through Google's User Messaging Platform and Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. See our Privacy Policy for details.

10. REAL-WORLD SAFETY

FanGeo helps users discover games, venues, and pickup activity, but we do not supervise in-person meetings. Before meeting others:

- Meet in public places
- Tell someone you trust where you are going
- Do not share sensitive personal or financial information with strangers
- Leave situations that feel unsafe

FanGeo is not an emergency service.

11. EMERGENCIES

If you or someone else is in immediate danger, contact local emergency services right away. FanGeo cannot replace police, medical, or crisis responders.
