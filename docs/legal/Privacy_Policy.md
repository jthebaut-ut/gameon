## Overview

FanGeo collects and uses information to provide sports discovery, social features, private messaging, venue and business tools, ads, safety reporting, and account management.

## Information We Collect

- Account and profile data, including email address, display name, username, bio, avatar, authentication identifiers, account status, and profile preferences.
- Location and discovery data, including device location if permission is granted, searched city or map region, venue coordinates, saved venues, saved games, attendance and interest signals, and local discovery activity.
- Fan preferences, including favorite teams, sports filters, live visibility settings, notification settings, and similar personalization choices.
- User-generated content, including Fan Chats, comments, direct messages, private conversation metadata, reports, venue/event posts, profile photos, venue photos, and business listing content.
- Safety and moderation data, including reports, selected report reasons, optional details, reported content, limited surrounding context where supported, moderation status, blocks, timestamps, and account identifiers.
- Venue and business claim data, including venue owner account data, business names, owner emails, claim details, location details, photos, game schedules, approval status, and related administrative records.
- Ads, analytics, diagnostics, and crash-related data used to operate, debug, secure, and improve the app.

## How We Use Information

We use information to authenticate accounts, show profiles, personalize sports and venue discovery, deliver DMs and Fan Chats, operate safety reporting and moderation, manage venue and business claims, provide ads, prevent abuse, troubleshoot crashes or reliability issues, comply with legal or App Store obligations, and improve FanGeo.

## Location Data

FanGeo may use location data to show nearby venues, games, fans, and local activity. You can control device location permissions in iOS Settings. FanGeo may still use city searches, selected map regions, or venue coordinates you interact with in the app.

## Ads and Third Parties

FanGeo uses Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may process device, ad interaction, advertising identifier, approximate location, and consent-related data according to your device settings and Google's policies.

FanGeo also uses service providers such as Supabase for authentication, database, storage, realtime features, and edge functions; Apple platform services; and sports data providers such as TheSportsDB for schedules and live match data.

## Reports and Moderation

When you report users, comments, messages, conversations, venues, or other content, FanGeo may review your report and related context. Moderation review may include limited reported content, account identifiers, timestamps, selected reasons, optional details, and moderation history.

## Account Deletion

Deleting your account removes or anonymizes your public profile and personal preferences. Some messages, comments, reports, and moderation records may be retained and shown as "Deleted User" to preserve conversation integrity, safety, and legal/compliance records.

Deleted accounts cannot log back in unless FanGeo support restores or reactivates the account.

## Data Retention

FanGeo keeps information while needed to operate the app, provide features, maintain safety, prevent abuse, resolve disputes, comply with legal or App Store obligations, and maintain records. Retention periods vary by data type. Shared content, reports, moderation records, venue/business records, and legal/compliance records may be retained after account deletion when necessary.
