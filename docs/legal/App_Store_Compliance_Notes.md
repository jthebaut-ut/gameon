## UGC and Social Safety

FanGeo includes UGC surfaces such as Fan Chats, comments, DMs, profiles, reports, venue/business listing content, and photos. In-app policy copy now explains acceptable use, harassment rules, report/block behavior, and moderation rights.

## Reporting and Blocking

FanGeo includes report paths for comments and private conversation safety surfaces where implemented. The Safety & Reporting copy explains that moderation review may include limited reported content and related metadata. Blocking is described for supported surfaces.

## Account Deletion

The Delete Account flow explains that profile/preferences are removed or anonymized, while chats, Fan Chat comments, reports, and moderation records may remain as "Deleted User" for conversation integrity, safety, and legal/compliance records. Deleted accounts are blocked from logging back in unless support restores or reactivates them.

## Ads and Third Parties

The Privacy Policy draft discloses Google AdMob, Supabase, Apple platform services, and sports data providers such as TheSportsDB.
