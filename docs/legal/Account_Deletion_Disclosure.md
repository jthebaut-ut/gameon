# FanGeo Account Deletion Disclosure

Last updated: May 22, 2026

Draft for App Store submission review. This is not legal advice.

## In-App Disclosure

Deleting your account removes or anonymizes your FanGeo profile and personal preferences. Favorites, saved venues, teams, and saved activity are removed. Existing chats and Fan Chat comments may remain as "Deleted User" to preserve conversation integrity, safety, and legal/compliance records. Deleted accounts cannot log back in unless FanGeo support restores or reactivates them. This action cannot be undone.

## What Is Removed or Anonymized

- Public profile identity and avatar.
- Favorite teams and saved venues.
- Going, Interested, and personal preference signals.
- Account-specific profile and discovery preferences.

## What May Remain

- Direct messages and private conversation threads, shown as "Deleted User" where applicable.
- Fan Chat comments and conversation context, shown as "Deleted User" where applicable.
- Reports, moderation records, report context, and safety/compliance records.
- Venue or business records where retention is needed for safety, claim integrity, compliance, or legal reasons.

## Login Blocking

After deletion, the account is blocked from logging back in. If a deleted account signs in, FanGeo shows: "This account has been deleted. Contact support if you believe this was a mistake." The app signs out and returns to the signed-out state.
